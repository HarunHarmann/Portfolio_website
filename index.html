About Me

Hello, I’m Harun — an AI Engineer passionate about building intelligent systems that create real-world impact. I recently graduated from Hacettepe University’s Artificial Intelligence Engineering program, and I enjoy combining curiosity, discipline, and creativity to develop data-driven solutions.

So far, I’ve gained hands-on experience in machine learning, computer vision, and data engineering through two internships and two part-time roles. I contributed to projects such as UAV visual navigation, multi-sensor pipelines, anomaly detection, data validation, and image classification at companies including HAVELSAN, TIGA Healthcare, Mastersoft, and Konumsal Information Systems. These experiences taught me how to take responsibility, collaborate effectively, and deliver results as part of a team.

Outside of engineering, I enjoy an active and balanced lifestyle — from tennis and volleyball to snowboarding, dancing, music, fitness, and traveling. I believe staying curious and energetic makes me a better engineer and teammate. My goal is to grow in a forward-thinking company where I can contribute, keep improving, and be part of a positive team environment.
