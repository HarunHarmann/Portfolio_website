About Me

Hello, I’m Harun — an AI Engineer passionate about building intelligent systems that create real-world impact. I recently graduated from Hacettepe University’s Artificial Intelligence Engineering program and I love turning ideas into data-driven products.

I’ve gained hands-on experience in machine learning, computer vision, and data engineering through two internships and two part-time roles. I contributed to UAV visual navigation, multi-sensor pipelines, anomaly detection, data validation, and image classification at HAVELSAN, TIGA Healthcare, Mastersoft, and Konumsal Information Systems — learning to take ownership, collaborate deeply, and ship results.

Outside of engineering, I keep an active, balanced lifestyle — tennis, volleyball, snowboarding, dancing, music, fitness, and traveling. Staying curious and energetic makes me a better engineer and teammate. I’m excited to grow in a forward-thinking company where I can contribute, learn continuously, and build meaningful products.
