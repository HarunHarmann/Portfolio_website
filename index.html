About Me

Hello! I'm Harun, an enthusiast in sports, travel, and harmonized work with life. I am studying Artificial Intelligence Engineering at Hacettepe University. I have experience in machine learning, deep learning, and data analysis, and I love solving real-world problems with technology.

I worked on different projects during my internships. At Konumsal Bilgi Sistemleri, I developed an image classification project using TensorFlow and CNN and integrated it into a live website. At MasterSoft, I worked on a cybersecurity project and created a system to detect anomalies in web logs. Now, I am learning more about data engineering at TIGA Healthcare Technologies, focusing on ETL processes and data quality with Microsoft Azure.

I enjoy joining competitions and learning new things. For example, I participated in Teknofest AI in Transportation and Iyzico/Coderspace Datathon, where I improved my skills in computer vision and time series analysis.

Outside of work, I like staying active and exploring new places. I play volleyball and tennis every week and go to fitness sessions regularly. I also love traveling to different cities and countries to see new cultures. Watching movies is another hobby that helps me relax and find inspiration.

I believe in working hard, staying curious, and using AI to make life better for everyone.
